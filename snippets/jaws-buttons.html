I can only repro this sometimes. I have not found a way to do it deterministically.

Repro steps:

  1. INSERT+F3
  2. Choose "Buttons"
  3. Choose "First Button"
  4. Hit enter
  5. Observe JAWS reads "First"
  6. INSERT+F3
  7. Choose "Buttons"
  8. Choose "Second Button"
  9. Hit enter

Expected: JAWS reads "Second"

Actual: JAWS reads "First Second"

Alternate button navigation method: Use B and Shift+B to move between buttons. Or use tab and shift+tab to move the focus. These methods do not seem to ever trigger the behavior.
